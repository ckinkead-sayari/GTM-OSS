# Business Case Template

The deliverable that IS the sales process. Walk into the meeting with this filled in. The prospect evaluates their own data, not your product.

## Template

### Headline

> Because of [trigger event], [Company] should [recommended approach] by [deadline]. After, [Company] will avoid [negative outcomes] while unlocking [positive outcomes].

### Problem Statement

**Format A (emerging risk):**

> Every [frequency], at least [scope] are affected by [problem], costing [Company] [cost]. If not addressed by [deadline], [consequence].

**Format B (failed current approach):**

> Despite trying [failed solution], [Company] still can't [desired outcome] because [root cause], costing [Company] [cost].

### Recommended Approach

> [Solution description] validated through [X] engagements across [Y] companies in [sector]. For it to work: (1) [prerequisite], (2) [prerequisite], (3) [prerequisite].

### Target Outcomes

| Key Metric | Current State | Target by [Date] | Business Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Metric 1] | [From discovery] | [Target] | [Dollar/risk impact] |
| [Metric 2] | [From discovery] | [Target] | [Dollar/risk impact] |
| [Metric 3] | [From discovery] | [Target] | [Dollar/risk impact] |

### Required Investment

> [Engagement type + price]. [Client time commitment]. [Access/resources needed]. Timeline: [deliverable] within [timeframe].

## How to Fill It

- Scope, problem count, risk estimate → from your product/analysis
- Current state → from discovery call (prospect provides)
- Targets → from your analysis + benchmarks
- Price/timeline → from your pricing model
- Gaps in what you know = your discovery agenda

## Quality Bar

A C-minus (72%) business case delivered to every prospect beats a perfect one delivered to 8%. Presence beats perfection.
